How it works: Upload the Five9 daily report CSV for each day. The system reads each agent's login time and not-ready time, adds any manually coded non-system time, then deducts excess not-ready time (anything above 12.5% of total worked time). The resulting Final Hours are automatically written to the attendance sheet as that agent's worked hours for the day. You can replace a file at any time — previous data is overwritten.
